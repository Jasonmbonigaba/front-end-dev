Visit Musanze

Musanze, Rwanda Nature's Gateway. Nestled in the Virunga Mountains, it blends stunning landscapes with rich culture, serving as a base for gorilla trekking.

Interesting places to visit in Musanze

Vilunga Mountain Gorillas

Visiting gorillas in Rwanda offers close encounters with gorilla families in stunning mountainous terrain,providing a unique and unforgettable experience.

Bisate logde

A luxurious eco-friendly lodge, offering beautiful views of the Virunga Mountains and providing adventures in Volcanoes National Park.

lakes

Musanze features pictures que lakes like Ruhondo and Burera, offering beautiful scenery and opportunities for relaxation, boating, fishing, and birdwatching.
